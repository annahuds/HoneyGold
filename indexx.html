Our Story

Founded in 2020, HoneyGold is a family-owned bakery in the heart of the city. We’re passionate about hand-crafted baked goods made with love, tradition, and the freshest ingredients.

From flaky croissants to gooey cinnamon rolls, every bite is a celebration.
